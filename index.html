12-Week Rugby Off-Season
High / Low Training Method · Back Three & Centres
12 weeks 6 days/week Back three · Centres Advanced Full gym
High stress — heavy, explosive, maximal
Low stress — aerobic, technical, <80%
Key rules
⚡High days: 3–5min full rest between speed reps. Quality, not quantity.
❤️Low days: stay below 80% on all lifts. Protect your CNS.
🌙Sunday is always OFF. Non-negotiable.
📈Progress 2.5–5% intensity per week within each phase.
😴Sleep 8hrs minimum. Most gains happen off the field.
🍖Eat to perform. High days = high calories. Low days = maintenance.
